Library Conduct

Rules & Guidelines

01

Silence & Discipline

Maintain silence in all reading areas. Mobile phones must be on silent mode. Group discussions only in designated zones.

02

Borrowing Policy

Students: 3 books for 7 days. Faculty: 5 books for 30 days. Books must be returned or renewed before the due date.

03

Fine Structure

Overdue fine: ₹10 per day per book for students. Fines are automatically calculated and visible in your dashboard.

04

Reprography Policy

Xerox, Print and Scan is for book chapters and journal articles only. Not available for personal documents or assignments.

05

ID Card Mandatory

College ID card is mandatory to enter and access library services. No proxy entries. Lost card must be reported immediately.

06

Digital Resource Access

IEEE, Springer, Elsevier and Knimbus resources are for academic use only. Bulk downloading is strictly prohibited.

Ask Library AI

Instant answers, available 24×7

How many books can I borrow and for how long? ▾
Students are allowed to borrow up to 3 books for 7 days. Faculty members may borrow up to 5 books for 30 days. Books can be renewed online or at the counter, provided no other member has reserved that title. Overdue fine is ₹10 per day per book for students.
How do I access IEEE Xplore or Springer from home? ▾
Visit griet.knimbus.com and log in with your GRIET institutional email and password. Once logged in, you can access all subscribed databases including IEEE Xplore, SpringerLink, J-Gate and more — 24×7 from any device, anywhere in the world.
What are the library's opening hours? ▾
Regular hours are Monday – Friday: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM and Saturday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM. The library is closed on Sundays. During examination seasons, the library extends its hours to 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
How can I search for a specific book in the catalogue? ▾
Visit griet.bestbookbuddies.com to access the online catalogue. You can search by keyword, author, title, subject or ISBN. The system shows real-time availability, shelf location (rack number) and lets you place a hold request if the book is currently issued.
Is the reprography facility available for all documents? ▾
The Reprography facility (Xerox, Print, Scan) using the Xerox WorkCentre 5325 is available for book chapters and journal articles only. It is not available for personal documents, assignments or other non-academic materials. The service is available during library hours.
How do I check my fine or overdue status? ▾
Log in to the library portal using your College ID. Your personal dashboard shows active borrows, due dates, fine amount (if any) and your complete borrowing history. Fines are calculated automatically at ₹10 per day per book for students.
Can I get plagiarism checking for my thesis? ▾
Yes! GRIET Library provides plagiarism checking services via Turnitin. Contact the library staff at library@griet.ac.in to schedule a session. We also assist with citation guidance, bibliography formatting and APA/IEEE referencing for thesis and research papers.
