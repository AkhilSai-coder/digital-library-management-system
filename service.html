Help
Frequently Asked Questions
How many books can I borrow at once? +
Students can borrow up to 3 books for 7 days. Faculty members can borrow up to 5 books for 30 days.
What is the fine for overdue books? +
The fine is ₹10 per day per overdue book. Fines are calculated automatically and visible in your student dashboard.
How do I access IEEE or Springer from home? +
Log in to Knimbus Remote Access at griet.knimbus.com using your GRIET institutional credentials to access all subscribed databases.
Can I request a book not available in the library? +
Yes! Use the Book Request feature in the student portal. Your request will be reviewed by the librarian and you'll be notified.
How do I change my library password? +
Login → Dashboard → Change Password in the sidebar. Enter your current password and set a new one.
