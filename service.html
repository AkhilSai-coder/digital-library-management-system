Help
Frequently Asked Questions
How many books can I borrow at once?+
Students can borrow up to 3 books for 7 days. Faculty members can borrow up to 5 books for 30 days. Books can be renewed online if not reserved by someone else.
What is the fine for overdue books?+
The fine is ₹10 per day per book. Fines are calculated automatically and visible in your student dashboard. Pay before borrowing new books.
How do I access IEEE or Springer from home?+
Log in to Knimbus Remote Access at griet.knimbus.com using your GRIET institutional email and password. All subscribed databases are accessible 24×7 from anywhere.
Can I request a book not in the library?+
Yes. Use the Book Request feature in the student portal. Your request is reviewed by the librarian and you will be notified when the book is acquired or available.
What is the reprography facility and what is allowed?+
We have a Xerox WorkCentre 5325 for Print, Scan and Xerox. It is available only for library book chapters and journal articles. Personal documents, assignments and question papers are not permitted.
How do I find a textbook for my course?+
Use the Textbook Finder on this page — select your department, year and subject. You can also search the OPAC at griet.bestbookbuddies.com for real-time availability and shelf location.
