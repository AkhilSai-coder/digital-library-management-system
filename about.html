About Inspirer and Promoters

The Inspirer

Late Shri Gokaraju Rangaraju garu is the inspiration for establishment of Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society. He served as Member of Legislative Assembly from Undi Constituency in United Andhra Pradesh. He served as the Chairman of Board of Trustees of Tirumala Tirupathi Devasthanam twice. A crusader of education, he also served as Chairman of Governing Bodies of DNR College and SRKR Engineering College of West Godavari Districts, Andhra Pradesh.

The Promoters

Dr Gokaraju Ganga Raju is an Indian politician and Member of Parliament to the 16th Lok Sabha from Narsapuram, Andhra Pradesh in 2014. He is also the founder of Laila Group of Companies. He served as Secretary of Andhra Cricket Association (2001–2019) and Vice-President, South Zone, BCCI (2015–2019).

The Sponsoring Society

Dr Gokaraju Gangaraju garu started Gokaraju Educational Society in fond memory of his father Late Shri Gokaraju Rangaraju garu with a motto to “Promote modern and scientific education and to help develop the character of the younger generation of all castes, communities and religions”.

About GRIET

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET), established in 1997 under the patronage of the Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society, is an AICTE approved and permanently affiliated college with UGC Autonomous status under JNTUH, Hyderabad. Following its mission to achieve and impart quality education with emphasis on practical skills and social relevance, GRIET has created a conducive teaching learning environment supported by qualified faculty, modern infrastructure and strong academic support systems.

GRIET is accredited by NAAC with A++ Grade (CGPA: 3.55). Six UG programs and four PG programs are accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The institution is ISO 9001:2015 certified, GRMTL is accredited by NABL, and GRES is recognized as SIRO by DSIR, Government of India.

The institute is ranked in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) within the 101–150 rank band (2024 & 2023) and has consistently maintained strong national positions over the years.

Spread across 24 acres of lush green campus, the institution focuses on preparing engineering graduates with the right attitude, skill and knowledge aligned with industry and societal needs.

The institute provides state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, high-end computer centres, language labs, training and placement cell, NSS, gym, medical centre, solar rooftop systems, RO plant, STP and Divyangjan-friendly infrastructure.

The GRIET Library and Information Centre houses over 17,195 titles and 1,30,740 volumes, along with 150 print journals, 6500+ e-books and 21500+ e-journals. The library is fully automated using KOHA software with cloud-based OPAC.

GRIET has established advanced research facilities including AICTE IDEA Lab, Incubation Centre under MSME, High Performance Computing, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Centres, and DST-FIST sponsored SEM facility worth ₹1.2 Crores.

The institute holds MoUs with premier institutions and industries including IIT Hyderabad, NIT Warangal, Curtin University (Malaysia) and Infosys Spring Board.
