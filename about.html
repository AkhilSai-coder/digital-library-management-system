Library Overview
GRIET Central Library

The GRIET Central Library & Information Centre, established in 1997, is the intellectual heart of Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology. Spread across multiple floors of a dedicated building, it houses over 1,30,741 volumes of print books, 6,396 eBooks and 21,659 eJournals.

Fully automated using the KOHA Integrated Library Management System, it offers seamless access to OPAC, catalogue, issue-return and digital resources from any device, anytime — on campus or remotely via Knimbus.

Accredited by NAAC with A++ grade, the library is a member of DELNET and INFLIBNET N-LIST, providing access to millions of research articles and e-resources.

Library Conduct
Rules & Regulations
01

Silence & Discipline

Maintain silence in all reading areas. Mobile phones on silent mode. Group discussions only in designated zones.

02

Borrowing Policy

Students: 3 books for 7 days. Faculty: 5 books for 30 days. Renew before due date to avoid fines.

03

Fine Structure

Overdue fine: ₹10 per day per book. Fines auto-calculated and visible in your library dashboard.

04

Reprography Policy

Xerox, Print and Scan for books & journal articles only. Not for personal documents or assignments.

05

ID Card Mandatory

College ID required to enter and access all services. No proxy entries. Report lost cards immediately.

06

Digital Resource Access

IEEE, Springer, Elsevier and Knimbus for academic use only. Bulk or systematic downloading strictly prohibited.

07

Book Handling

Handle books with care. Do not write, underline or damage library materials. Damage charges apply.

08

Personal Belongings

Personal books and bags to be deposited at the entrance. Library is not responsible for lost items.
