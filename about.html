Our Story
GRIET Central Library

The GRIET Central Library, established in 1997, is the intellectual heart of Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology. Spread across multiple floors of a dedicated building, it houses over 1,30,000 volumes of print books, along with a rich digital library of eBooks and eJournals.

Our library is fully automated using the KOHA integrated library management system, offering seamless access to OPAC, catalogue, issue-return and digital resources from any device.

Accredited by NAAC with 'A++' grade, the library continuously evolves to meet the information needs of students, faculty and researchers across all disciplines.

  • NAAC A++ Accredited Institution
  • KOHA Automated Library Management
  • IEEE, Springer, Elsevier Database Access
  • Ranked Top 5 Library in Telangana
